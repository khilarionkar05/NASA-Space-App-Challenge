What is BloomWatch?

BloomWatch uses satellite vegetation indices (NDVI) to indicate when and where vegetation is greening up â€” a sign of seasonal leafing or mass flowering events.

How it works

  1. Satellites (MODIS, VIIRS, Landsat) measure reflected light to compute NDVI.
  2. Data processed in Google Earth Engine to detect sudden NDVI increases (bloom intensity).
  3. Results are displayed as map overlays and downloadable images.
